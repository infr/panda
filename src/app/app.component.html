Habitat information

  • Pandas are found from China and they live in a few mountain ranges in central China, mainly in Sichuan, but also in neighbouring Shaanxi and Gansu.
  • Panda's diet is primarily herbivorous, consisting almost exclusively of bamboo
  • Pandas communicate through vocalization and scent marking such as clawing trees or spraying urine.
  • A panda's daily diet consists almost entirely of the leaves, stems and shoots of various bamboo species.
  • Bamboo contains very little nutritional value so pandas must eat 12-38kg every day to meet their energy needs.
